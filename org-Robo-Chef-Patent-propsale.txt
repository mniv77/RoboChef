The Patent:   
The robotic machine will only accept food that displays an authorized encrypted code [AEC].
The AEC could be in machine-readable form.
 E.g., barcode, embedded chip, RF, etc.
The encrypted key will be changed daily by a random code genrator.
The decrypted information will display vital information about the food. 
Vendors of food supplies will be able to buy codes.
The AEC will be generated by the enterprise that has the right to the RoboChef machine patent. Selling the AEC will provide income to the patent holder of the RoboChef.
